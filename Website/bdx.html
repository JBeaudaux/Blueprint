Julien Beaudaux, Ph.D

  • # Role in the project: Creator and contributor
  • # Topics of interest: Embedded systems, Internet of things, Telemedicine and home care
  • # Contact: julienbeaudaux -at- gmail -dot- com
  • # Curriculum vitae:

After obtaining a Master degree (MSc) in computer networks and embedded systems, he received his Ph.D degree from the University of Strasbourg, France, in 2013. His interests include wireless sensors, the Internet of things (IoT), and their applications in the fields of telemedicine and home-care. He has authored and co-authored over 10 scientific papers in these fields.

After working at he NTNU Trondheim (Norway), in collaboration with St. Olavs Hospital, on solutions to better monitor and analyse behavioral and environmental metrics impacting health, he now designs defibrilators and heart-monitoring solutions at Schiller Medical in Alsace (France). He is also involved in several open projects, including the IoT-LAB experimentation platform for the Internet of things and the TAMIAS open-source privacy-aware storage system.
