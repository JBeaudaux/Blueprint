Blue print, in a few words

Health is our most precious gift, but also the most fragile one. Indeed, noone is safe from the sudden deterioration of its health condition. For instance, heart diseases (e.g. strokes, ischaemic heart diseases) may impact anyone and cause about 25% of total death, and many elders have experienced critical situations (e.g. falls, loss of points of reference) without being able to call for help. Such events are difficult to anticipate long in advance through regular medical check-ups, and may lead to severe and irreversible consequences in ones health.

Yet, simple little steps can be followed to take back your life in hand and prevent most potential at-risk events. The non-obtrusive monitoring of a few health parameters (e.g. heart rate and variation, movement) or the follow-up of life-style and daily activity can be sufficient to anticipate and address in time most of the aforementioned critical situations. In this context, the Blue print project aims at helping you take your health in hand and reduce your risk level, step by step, by offering open, efficient and non-obtrusive solutions to monitor, process and analyse key health parameters.

Who is Blue print for?

# Healthy individuals looking for solutions to better manage their health relying on evidence.
# People at risk willing to obtain relevant advises to reduce their risk-level and anticipate critical situations.
# Health & care services aiming at remotely monitoring their patient, either for scientific studies or medical care purposes.
# Companies involved in health monitoring building healthcare solutions based on lifestyle and enviromental parameters.

Blue print, it is also:

  • # An open project. All the source code and documentation is openly available and verifyable by anyone, at anytime.
  • # A wide range of life-logging and analysis solutions, non-obtrusive, modular and within reach of all.
  • # Passionate people, providing the community with relevant tools for improving people's health.
